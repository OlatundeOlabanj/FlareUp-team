Frequently Asked Questions

Is FlareUp free to use?

Yes! FlareUp offers a free plan with all the essential features to create and share your digital Flare. We also offer premium plans with additional features for professionals who want more customization and analytics.

What makes it different from other platforms or a portfolio site?

FlareUp combines the best aspects of professional networks and portfolio sites while eliminating their limitations. Unlike other platforms, we focus on showcasing your actual work and skills rather than just your employment history. Unlike traditional portfolio sites, we make it incredibly easy to create and optimize your presence while connecting you with real opportunities.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We take data privacy and security very seriously. Your information is encrypted, and we never sell your data to third parties. You have full control over what information you share publicly.

Can I use it from my phone?

Yes! FlareUp is fully responsive and works beautifully on mobile devices. We also offer iOS and Android apps for an even better mobile experience.

Do I need tech skills?

Not at all! FlareUp is designed to be intuitive and easy to use for everyone, regardless of technical ability. Our drag-and-drop editor and pre-designed templates make creating your Flare simple and fun.
